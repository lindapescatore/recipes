How to make microwave popcorn:

1. Remove any clear plastic overwrap.
2. Place paper bag in microwave, ensuring that the side marked 'this side up' faces up.
3. Close the door and press the button marked 'Popcorn.'
4. Listen for popping sounds. Stay nearby and monitor the process. If the microwave doesn't turn off before the pops stop, turn it off.
5. Remove the bag and open it carefully, as steam will escape. 
6. Enjoy your microwave popcorn!
